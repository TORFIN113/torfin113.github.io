Our Mission

We build software that matters

At Torfin, we believe that great software has the power to transform businesses, improve lives, and shape the future. Founded in India, we started as a small team of passionate engineers who wanted to do software differently — with craft, transparency, and genuine client partnership.

Today, we serve clients across industries, building everything from consumer apps to enterprise platforms — while simultaneously developing our own products that we're proud to put our name on. Both sides of our work make us better: client projects sharpen our delivery skills, and product work gives us a builder's perspective.

Our Story

From a small team to a growing studio

2025

Founded in India

Torfin was started by a group of engineers who shared one simple belief: software should be built right, not just built fast. We took on our first two freelance projects with a handshake and a deadline.

2025

First Major Client Project

We delivered a full-stack SaaS platform for a logistics startup in Hyderabad — our first six-figure project. This validated our approach to technical excellence and client communication.

2025

Launched Vanaraksha Internally

Frustrated with existing project tools, we built Vanaraksha for our own team. Clients started asking to use it too — and our products division was born.

2026

Expanded to Mobile & AI

Added Flutter development and AI-integration capabilities to our service offering. Delivered 18 projects that year alone, including two mobile apps now live in the Google Play Store.

2026 →

Growing Our Product Suite

StudyHive launched publicly. Revise Buddy entered beta. We're now a team of 12, serving clients globally while continuing to build and ship products under the Torfin brand.
